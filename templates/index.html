🦸 Super Air 🌬️ Check!

Research question: How does indoor air quality in different rooms of my home compare in the morning, at lunch, and at night? I measure with a BME680 sensor and record a 0–100 “freshness” score. I select the room on this page each time I move the Raspberry Pi, and the date and clock on the Pi put each sample into morning, lunch, or night. The lights on my board follow live readings: green = good, yellow = in the middle, red = it’s a good time for fresh air.

Change this every time you move the box to a new place.
